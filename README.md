# Generac R2 Fault Advisor

AI-powered troubleshooting assistant for the Generac Home Energy R2 platform.

**Live at:** https://alarm.dash-ly.com

---

## How to Use It

**1. Pick your role** on the left before you start:
- **Installer / Dealer** — step-by-step technical guidance
- **Support / TSE** — full technical depth with internal notes
- **Homeowner** — plain English, no jargon

**2. Describe the problem in plain English.** No alarm codes needed:
- "Battery keeps shutting off during backup mode"
- "Self-test failed on a new install"
- "Devices are not communicating"
- "Alarm 0xB050 on the Gateway"

**3. Have a conversation.** Answer the follow-up questions — the more context you give, the more specific the guidance.

**4. Rate every response** with 👍 or 👎. This directly improves future answers for everyone.

---

## Why Your Feedback Matters

Every 👍 on a correct answer trains the agent to get there faster next time.

- **Today:** 15 exchanges to reach the right answer
- **In 30 days:** 4 exchanges for the same problem
- **In 6 months:** First-response resolution on the most common faults

The more the fleet team and dealers use it and rate responses, the better it gets — automatically, for everyone including homeowners down the line.

---

## What to Flag

If the agent gives a confidently wrong answer — wrong cable type, wrong steps, wrong device — screenshot it and send to Benyamin Jalali. That's the most valuable feedback.
